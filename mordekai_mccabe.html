Mordekai McCabe

Level 7 Human Fighter

AC 23, touch 13, flat-footed 20 (+10 armor, +2 Dex, +1 natural)

hp 82 (7d10+28)

Fort +9, Ref +5, Will +3

Speed 20 ft.

Melee Fulgur +12/+7 (2d8+6 plus 1d6 electricity)

Ranged Drake Bone LongBow +10/+5 (1d8/x3)

Str 18, Dex 14, Con 14, Int 12, Wis 10, Cha 12

Base Atk +7; CMB +11; CMD 23

Feats: Weapon Focus (Longsword), Greater Weapon Focus (Longsword), Weapon Specialization (Longsword), Greater Weapon Specialization (Longsword), Toughness, Improved Initiative

Feats Explanation:

  • Weapon Focus (Longsword): +1 bonus on all attack rolls with longsword
  • Greater Weapon Focus (Longsword): An additional +1 bonus on attack rolls with longsword (total of +2)
  • Weapon Specialization (Longsword): +2 bonus on all damage rolls with longsword
  • Greater Weapon Specialization (Longsword): An additional +2 bonus on damage rolls with longsword (total of +4)
  • Toughness: +7 hit points
  • Improved Initiative: +4 bonus on initiative checks

Skills: Acrobatics +11, Bluff +11, Craft (blacksmithing) +21, Knowledge (local) +11, Perception +10, Swim +12

Languages: Common

Gear: Fulgur, Drake Bone Longbow with 20 arrows, Adamantine Scale Mail, potions of cure moderate wounds (3)

Combat Behavior:

Mordekai prefers to start combat with a surprise attack using his longbow. Once in melee, he uses Fulgur to deliver powerful and precise strikes, leveraging his feats to maximize his effectiveness with this weapon. His Improved Initiative allows him to act quickly in combat, often getting the first strike. His Toughness feat helps him withstand more damage in battle, making him a sturdy front-line combatant.
