Billy Cottrell: Level 6 Human Champion (Paladin)

Perception +10; Alignment Lawful Good; Size Medium; Speed 25 feet

Ability Scores Str 18, Dex 12, Con 14, Int 10, Wis 14, Cha 16

Skills Athletics +13, Diplomacy +12, Religion +10, Survival +10, Farming Lore +9

AC 24 (26 with shield raised); HP 80; Fort +12, Ref +9, Will +11; Resistances +1 status to all saves vs. spells

Champion's Code

Billy follows the tenets of good and law, and he loses his divine ally and righteous ally reactions if he violates these tenets.

Actions

Melee [one-action] bastard sword +13 (versatile P, two-hand d12), Damage 2d8+4 slashing

Ranged [one-action] longbow +11 (range increment 100 feet, reload 0), Damage 2d8+4 piercing

Retributive Strike [reaction] (divine, abjuration, champion, lawful, good) Trigger: An enemy damages an ally within 15 feet. Effect: Billy makes a Strike against the triggering enemy at a -2 penalty. If the Strike hits, the ally gains resistance to all damage against the triggering damage equal to 2 + his level.

Raise Shield [one-action]: +2 bonus to AC until the start of his next turn.

Shield Block [reaction]: Reduce damage from a physical attack by 5 once per round, but the shield takes the remaining damage.
